Frequently Asked Questions

What are the admission requirements? +
You need a high school diploma with minimum 75% marks and valid entrance exam scores.
Are scholarships available? +
Yes! We offer merit-based scholarships covering up to 100% of tuition fees.
Do you have hostel facilities? +
Yes, we have separate hostels for boys and girls with modern amenities.
